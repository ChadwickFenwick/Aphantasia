UI/UX Style Guide: "Monocle"1. Design PhilosophyFocus-Centric: Zero clutter. Every element should lead the eye toward the visualization target.Ethereal & Fluid: Use subtle blurs and gradients to mimic the "foggy" nature of early-stage mental imagery.Tactile: Buttons and cards should feel like physical glass or "soft" plastic (Glassmorphism).2. Visual Effects & AnimationsThe "Dissolve" Transition: When an image disappears for the afterimage exercise, use a 1.5s ease-out opacity fade.Background Noise: Apply a very subtle grain/noise SVG filter (opacity 0.03) to the entire background to prevent "banding" on OLED screens and give it a "textured" feel.Glow Trails: Use Framer Motion or GSAP to create a faint "trail" effect when the user moves their mouse/finger over the canvas.6.
