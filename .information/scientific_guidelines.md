Habituation Prevention (The "Surprise" Factor)The brain ignores what is predictable.Guideline: Randomized Stimuli.Implementation: Never show the same shape in the same color twice in a row. Use a randomizer for the Three.js geometry to keep the visual cortex "surprised" and engaged.6.
